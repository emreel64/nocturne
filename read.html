About Nocturne

Nocturne is a student-run magazine out of Özel İzmir Amerikan Koleji.

We write about whatever we can't stop thinking about — films and music we keep coming back to, books we won't shut up about, the strange small things about growing up in İzmir, the people who made the work we love. Mostly the stuff that keeps us up.

Two issues in. Run by students, for students, with an editorial standard somewhere between broadsheet and group chat. We publish when we have something worth saying, and we'd rather be quiet than dilute it.

If you've got something to say — a piece, a photograph, an interview, an axe to grind — the tab is right there. We're always reading.
